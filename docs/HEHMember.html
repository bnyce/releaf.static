Membership at ReLeaf

Join the Movement, Grow with Us

Why Become a Member?

Becoming a member of ReLeaf doesn't just mean joining a cooperative; it means becoming part of a vibrant community of creatives, environmentalists, and changemakers. Here, you'll find a space to explore Organic Media, contribute to your local Watersheds, build Little Free Libraries, and so much more.

Absolutely Free Participation

At ReLeaf, we believe in the freedom of expression and the freedom of participation. Whether you're joining us in Marigold Town or from anywhere in the world, remember: participation is always free. We invite you to bring your passion, creativity, and innovation to the table, without any financial barriers.

The Power of Organic Media

Organic Media is at the heart of what we do. Just like nature, it replicates freely and adapts to its environment. By promoting Organic Media, we aim to inspire you to play with reality and contribute to the world in your unique way.

Get Involved

Are you ready to take the next step? From in-person meetings to online collaborations, there are countless opportunities to get involved with ReLeaf. Coordinate with us in real life, join our online forums, or take part in our creative endeavors.

Contact Us

If you're interested in getting involved, contributing, or even bringing up a tangential project you think would be of interest, we invite you to reach out directly to our founder, Bryce. Feel free to email bryceb@releaf.site to discover how you can become a part of this incredible journey.
