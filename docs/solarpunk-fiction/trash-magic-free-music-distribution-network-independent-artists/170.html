Trash Magic: Free Music Distribution Network for Independent Artists

SolarPunk Fiction
by trashrobot

Hey there musicians! Have you ever felt like the traditional recording and distribution systems don't benefit you as an artist? We have an alternative solution! Trash Magic is a network of operators who take donated computers and use them to distribute music for free. We believe that fans should support musicians by supporting them as artists, not just by buying the rights to their music. And the best part? The hardware and software are both in the public domain! We would love for you to donate your songs to the public domain so that we can create self-replicating mixtapes that use your music. Just send us your song or a link to it, along with a statement releasing it into the public domain. We'll create a Trash Magic web page for you with all of your information, links to your social media and payment platforms, and any other art you want to share. Welcome to Trash Magic! Let's build a new world out of self-replicating media made from trash! Tell your story and why people should listen to your music and support your work. We even have a web code editor and a QR code generator to make sharing your mixtape even easier. Join us in creating something truly unique and revolutionary!
