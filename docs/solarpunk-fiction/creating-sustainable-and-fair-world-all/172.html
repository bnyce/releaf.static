Creating a Sustainable and Fair World for All

SolarPunk Fiction
by bryceb

Hey there! We want everyone in the world to have a happy life with lots of good things. We have all the things we need to make this happen, like energy that doesn't cost too much, lots of food, and ways to get rid of waste safely. But sometimes we don't have enough of these things and it makes us sad. We want to figure out why this happens and how we can use things we already have to make sure everyone has enough. Right now, some people have more things than others and that's not fair. We need to find a new way to share things so everyone can have what they need. It's like when you share your toys with your friends so everyone can have fun together. We can't keep doing things the way we are because it's not good for the planet and it makes people unhappy. We want to find a better way so everyone can be happy!
