ORGANIC MEDIA COLLECTIONS and BOTANICAL GARDENS

Welcome to The ReLeaf Organic Media Collections and Botanical Gardens in Marigold Town. There are 150 acres of gardens for you to explore, and the ReLeaf is also home to a world-class organic media collection and a renowned library of rare books and manuscripts. The ReLeaf is open Tuesday through Friday, noon to 4:30 p.m.; Saturday and Sunday, 10:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Summer hours: Tuesday to Sunday, 10:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Information line: (123) 456-7890.
