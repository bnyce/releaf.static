Welcome to the enthralling virtual reality tour of the still-intact time capsule from 1998, discovered on Rio Grande Street and inscribed with the words "Pease Elementary." This extraordinary capsule is a frozen moment in time, holding within it the memories, culture, and perhaps even untold secrets of a past era. As you immerse yourself in this VR journey, you'll gain exclusive access to what life was like over two decades ago, specifically from the perspective of the Pease Elementary community. This isn't just a collection of objects; it's a narrative thread linking past, present, and future in a captivating tale that awaits your discovery. Prepare to traverse the borders of time as you delve into the mysteries contained in this remarkable capsule.
