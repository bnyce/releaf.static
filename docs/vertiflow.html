VertiFlow

P.A.R.T.S.

  • Bag of aluminum cans
    • VertiCan

The VertiFlow system consists of a linear arrangement of VertiCans that are strung together in a vertical series. It is an excellent option for attaching to a fence post or positioning beneath a gutter. This system takes advantage of natural rainfall to provide moisture to each planter module. The combination of upcycling and rainwater harvesting makes VertiFlow an eco-conscious choice for vertical gardening.

Optimally, the VertiFlow system employs a long strip of upcycled fabric, which serves as a central, spine-like wick. These are often colloquially referred to as "Wick-ed VertiFlow creek sippers".

The VertiFlow system, while highly effective and eco-conscious on its own, can be further improved with the addition of an EcoSleeve. The EcoSleeve, typically fashioned from environmentally-friendly materials, works to enhance the wicking process, ensuring each planter module receives adequate moisture. This addition also acts as a cover for the system, thereby reducing the industrial look of the exposed metal, and adding an aesthetically pleasing touch to your vertical garden.

Moreover, spiraling strips of fabric can be added to the VertiFlow system. These not only improve the wicking process but also contribute to a more visually appealing setup. Both EcoSleeve and spiraling fabric strips help refine the VertiFlow system, improving its functionality while ensuring it blends seamlessly into any outdoor decor.
