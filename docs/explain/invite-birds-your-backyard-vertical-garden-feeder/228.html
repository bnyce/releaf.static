Invite Birds to Your Backyard with a Vertical Garden Feeder

Explain Like I'm Five
by bryceb

Have you ever seen a bird in your backyard? They are really cool and help our environment. There are special things called bird feeders that we can put outside to invite more birds to hang out with us. They help make our backyard pretty and fun to watch. But there is a new kind of bird feeder that is even better! It's called a vertical garden and it's made just for birds. It has lots of different plants that birds like to eat and play in. It even has a place for them to drink and take a bath! When we have more birds in our yard, they can help us by eating bugs and helping plants grow. ReLeaf made these special gardens and they are helping lots of birds come live in the city. It's really neat to see all the different kinds of birds that come visit. We can help the environment and make our backyard look pretty at the same time!
