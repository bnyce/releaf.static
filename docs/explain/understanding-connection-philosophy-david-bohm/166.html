Understanding Connection: The Philosophy of David Bohm

Explain Like I'm Five
by bryceb

Let's talk about a really smart and cool guy named David Bohm. He studied things like science and philosophy and wanted to understand how everything in the world is connected. He thought that instead of thinking of everything as separate, we should think of everything as one big thing that's always moving and changing. This way of thinking can help us be nicer to each other and take care of our planet better. We can even make gardens in unexpected places to show how everything is connected! David Bohm's ideas can help us understand how our thoughts and feelings are connected to the world around us too. He wanted people to think in a new way and see that everything is part of one big, amazing thing.
