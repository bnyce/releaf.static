Explain Like I'm Five
by bryceb

There is a group called ReLeaf that is really good at making waste into something useful. They take things that people don't need anymore and turn them into beautiful gardens! These gardens are very tall and can be seen from far away. They make the city look really pretty and help the environment. ReLeaf also makes sure that everyone who helps them gets rewarded for their hard work. They want everyone to be happy and work together to make the city a better place. ReLeaf is like a treasure map that helps us find new and exciting places in the city. They are doing a great job and we can learn a lot from them!
