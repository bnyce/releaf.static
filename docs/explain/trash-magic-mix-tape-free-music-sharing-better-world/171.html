Hey there kiddo! Let me tell you about Trash Magic Mix Tape! It's all about sharing music with others for free. Instead of buying music, people can donate old computers to help us share music. We ask musicians to share their songs with us so we can make mix tapes to share with others. We don't want to own the music, we just want to support the artists who make it. We'll make a special web page for each artist with their music, social media links, and payment information. And we'll make it easy for people to listen to your music and support you. Trash Magic is all about making a new world out of old things and sharing music with others. We can even make special codes for people to use at concerts to share mix tapes with their friends. So let's make some music and share it with the world!
