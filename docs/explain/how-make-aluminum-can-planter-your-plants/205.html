How to Make an Aluminum Can Planter for Your Plants

Explain Like I'm Five
by bryceb

Hey there! Do you want to make a cool planter for your plants? Here's how you can do it with an aluminum can! First, you need to clean the can really well with soap and water. Then, you can make some holes in the bottom of the can so the water can drain out. But be careful and wear gloves so you don't hurt yourself! Now, if you want to make your planter even better, you can add some other things to it! You can put a string inside the can to help the water get to the roots of the plant. Or, you could connect a bunch of planters together so they all get watered at the same time. You can also make your planter look really pretty by wrapping it in fabric or painting it. And if you want to save space, you can even hang your planter on the wall with some hooks! Just remember, when you add stuff to your planter, make sure it's safe for your plants. With these steps, you can make an awesome planter that's good for the environment too!
